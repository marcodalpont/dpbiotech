Mobile Series

DP Mini

The first system to turn a smartphone into a true stereoscopic microscope. Using an innovative optical architecture, it captures 3D images from a single mobile sensor, offering unprecedented portability and quality.

  • Active stereoscopic system that acquires alternate images from two distinct optical paths using a single mobile sensor.
  • Compact optical architecture with a beam splitter and two electro-optical shutters to separate the channels.
  • Optional 90° viewing angle via a pentaprism, for ergonomics similar to a traditional microscope.
  • Full integration with Augmented and Mixed Reality to overlay 3D models and clinical data.

Digital Surgical Series

DP Pro

A revolutionary digital surgical microscope that redefines stability, control, and ergonomics. The optical body moves independently on a gimbal stabilization system, offering unparalleled precision.

  • Three-axis gimbal stabilization (roll, pitch, yaw) keeps the optical body perfectly steady, regardless of arm movements.
  • Articulated, extensible arm for flexible positioning without disturbing the patient.
  • Intuitive control via pressure sensors integrated into the oculars or a multifunction joystick.
  • Native integration with Artificial Intelligence for anatomical structure recognition and surgical guidance.
