The magic behind Fusion 3D

How does it create 3D with just one camera? DP Mini rapidly captures two separate views—one for your left eye and one for your right. It then instantly combines them into a single, fluid 3D video.

This smart process creates a realistic sense of depth, making your view clear, stable, and comfortable to watch.
