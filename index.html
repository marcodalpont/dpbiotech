Mobile Series

DP Mini

The first system to turn a smartphone into a true stereoscopic microscope. Using an innovative optical architecture, it captures 3D images from a single mobile sensor, offering unprecedented portability and quality.

  • Active stereoscopic system for true 3D imaging.
  • Compact, portable design for use anywhere.
  • Optional 90° viewing angle for ergonomic comfort.
  • Full integration with Augmented and Mixed Reality.

Digital Surgical Series

DP Pro

A revolutionary digital surgical microscope that redefines stability, control, and ergonomics. The optical body moves independently on a gimbal stabilization system, offering unparalleled precision.

  • Three-axis gimbal stabilization for a perfectly steady view.
  • Articulated, extensible arm for flexible positioning.
  • Intuitive control via joystick or pressure sensors.
  • Native integration with AI for surgical guidance.

Built for clinical use

From glass to mechanics, every component is tuned for stability, visibility and comfort during long procedures.

OP

Optics

Plan‑apochromatic lenses, AR‑coated, neutral colorimetry. Zoom 1:6. Eyepieces 10× or 12.5×.

WD

Working distance

Objectives f=175 / 200 / 250 mm to match your specialty and posture.

CX

Coaxial LED

High‑intensity LED (80–120 W) with homogeneous field and low heat.

ER

Ergonomics

Balanced arms, smooth friction, sterile‑drape ready handles and controls.

SP

Beam splitters

20/80 or 50/50 with dual C‑mount ports for camera/teaching.

AS

Assistant binocular module for training and hand‑offs.

MT

Mounting

Floor stand, ceiling mount or wall arm — same optical head.

ST

Sterility

Sterile handles and drapes available; easy to clean surfaces.
